Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2025

AuthorX Automation (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides automation workflows (e.g., via n8n) that may access Google services only with your explicit authorization through OAuth.

Data We Access

  • Only the Google data needed to perform the actions you authorize (e.g., reading email metadata, sending mail, reading/writing Drive files).
  • No selling or sharing of personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Use Data

  • To execute the workflows you initiate or configure.
  • To maintain service functionality and security.

Data Storage & Retention

  • We strive to store only what is required to complete automations (e.g., message IDs, workflow logs).
  • Refresh tokens (if used) are stored securely to maintain your connection and can be revoked at any time in your Google Account.

Revoking Access

You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account’s security settings.
